**Project:** EspaLuz - AI-Powered Emotional Language Learning Platform  
**Status:** Live & Operational | Solo-Built | Zero External Funding  
**Vision:** World's first emotionally intelligent language learning AI

## 💡 THE VISION

**Problem Solved:** Traditional language learning apps are cold, robotic, and don't understand the emotional context of real-life situations.

**Solution:** EspaLuz detects emotional states (frustration, embarrassment, breakthrough moments) and adapts responses with empathy, creating personalized motivational content for learners navigating real-world challenges.

**Market:** Expat families, digital nomads, service providers, healthcare workers, entrepreneurs - anyone needing language skills for life-critical situations.
